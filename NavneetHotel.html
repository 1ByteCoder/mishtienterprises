Navneet Hotel

Navneet Hotel is the favoured choice for staying in Garhwa and it is situated at the prime location of the city. This Hotel is spread across an area of 8712 square ft (20 decmil), the hotel features 30 A.C/ non A.C rooms with including washrooms . This hotel provides a relaxed, festive ambiance with nearby restaurants. An ideal choice for staying. Navneet Hotel is easily accessible from Garhwa Railway Station.

Rooms are available at affordable price.

  2. Double Occupancy @ Rs 600 non A.C
  3. Single Occupancy @ Rs 1000 A.C
  4. Double Occupancy @ Rs 1200 A.C
